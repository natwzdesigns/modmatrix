// The Channel

About Mod Matrix

Mod Matrix is a Minecraft content project by ExoCubeYT — a YouTuber covering client reviews, texture pack reviews, and mod downloads across Java, Bedrock and MCPE. ExoCubeYT is also a co-founder of Lumina Client, an advanced Bedrock & MCPE utility client.

The site exists to give the community a clean, permanent place to find download links for every reviewed client — all in one place, with version history and platform info.

Over 100K+ downloads served through the channel and site combined.

// History

How it started

2022
Channel Launches

ExoCubeYT channel starts on YouTube. First Minecraft client reviews and download tutorials. Small but dedicated audience begins building.

2023
Lumina Client Co-Founded

ExoCubeYT becomes one of two founders of Lumina Client — an advanced Minecraft Bedrock utility client with cross-platform proxy support. Lumina quickly gains traction in the community.

2024
Scope Expands

Content grows from MCPE into Java and Bedrock. Lumina Client continues development and grows its user base. Focus expands to reviewing the best external clients across all three platforms.

2025
Mod Matrix Launches

The project gets a proper home — Mod Matrix. A full site covering Java, Bedrock and MCPE clients with version history, platform tags, and a clean download experience.
