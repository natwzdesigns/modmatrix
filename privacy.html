Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Mod Matrix collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

📅 Last updated: March 2026

🔒 Short version: Mod Matrix does not collect, store, or sell any personal data. We don't have accounts, logins, or user tracking beyond standard hosting analytics.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Mod Matrix does not require registration or login. We do not collect, store or process:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Account credentials of any kind
  • Payment or financial information
  • Chat logs or personal communications
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints

2. Hosting & Server Analytics

Like all websites, basic server-level data may be logged by our hosting provider. This typically includes IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. This data is used only for server performance and security monitoring, is not shared, and is not tied to any individual identity.

3. Link Shorteners

Download links on Mod Matrix may route through link shortener services (such as Linkvertise or similar). These third-party services may collect analytics data such as click counts, approximate location, and browser type to display ads and track performance. This data is collected by those third-party services — not by Mod Matrix — and is governed by their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any link shortener service you interact with.

4. Third-Party Links

Mod Matrix links to third-party websites including YouTube, Discord, GitHub, and download hosts. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any personal data to them.

5. Cookies

Mod Matrix does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Your browser may cache site assets (images, fonts, stylesheets) locally for performance — this is standard browser behaviour and not a tracking mechanism.

6. Children's Privacy

Mod Matrix does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. The Site is a general-purpose content resource. If you believe a child has submitted personal data to us, please contact us via Discord and we will address it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes take effect immediately upon being posted. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach out via the Mod Matrix Discord.
